LESS IS MORE

In the world of computer science and technology, simplicity is power. The principle of "Less is More" reminds us that clarity, efficiency, and focus often lead to the best results. In design, minimalism makes interfaces clean, intuitive, and user-friendly. In code, writing less—yet more efficient—lines improves readability, reduces bugs, and ensures scalability. In innovation, simplicity sparks creativity, helping us solve problems without unnecessary complexity. By embracing Less is More, we create technology that is not only functional but also elegant, sustainable, and impactful. My website reflects this belief: minimal in style, but meaningful in purpose.
